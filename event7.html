MUD MASH

About:

A part of SPE INDHAN 2023 , we invite you to participate in the contest of MUD MASH. The objective behind the contest is to enhance the experimental and immense technical knowledge in a laboratory .
Hence we are giving you a challenge to put your experimental knowledge and problem solving skills to a test.

Guidelines and rules:

  • Equipment should be carefully handled.
  • Lab etiquette must be followed.
  • The participant has to conduct their whole experiment with the mud that has been provided at the start.

Conduction criteria:

Participants are provided with prepared mud sample.They are required to determine the following drilling fluid properties :

1. Density of mud sample
2. Viscosity of mud sample
3. Resistivity of mud sample
The participant is required to solve a simple problem which requires the value that the participant determined earlier through the experimentation.

Registration Guidelines:

  • Registration for this event should be made besides basic registration.
  • Registration fee to participate in the contest is Rs. 100/- (1 participant)

    • The dead line for registration is: 23-03-2023
