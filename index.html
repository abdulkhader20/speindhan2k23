About INDHAN

SPE INDHAN 2K23 is a two-day National Technical Symposium organised by the Department of Petroleum and Petrochemical Engineering, University College of Engineering Kakinada (UCEK), JNTUK. It brings together students from across the country to compete, collaborate, and innovate in the fields of petroleum and chemical engineering.


Organised under the banner of the Society of Petroleum Engineers (SPE) Student Chapter, INDHAN 2K23 features technical events, workshops, spot games, and more — all designed to challenge and inspire the next generation of engineers.
