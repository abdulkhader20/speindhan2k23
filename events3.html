TITRO

About:

This event deals with the chemical titrations. Concentration is the major factor that is to be considered to prepare a perfect chemical. This is the most crucial part yet more interesting one Give a try to find the end point and gain some fun learning experience with us.

GuideLines:

All the chemicals and the equipment will be provided

Rules:

  • Each student will be provided with already prepared acid and base solutions
  • The student is required to titrate the acid with base till it gets neutralized
  • The neutralization point should be noted and submitted to the organizer.
  • The student who gets the answer accurately or closely to the actual answer is rewarded.
  • The time taken by the student to complete the experiment is also noted.
  • The time taken by the student is also taken into consideration in deciding the winner if more than one person submits the correct answer.

The dead line for registration is: 23-03-2023

Rewards:
