MEMORANDUM (Quiz)

About:

"Unleash the power of your brain, take the quiz, and leave your reign!"

This competition furnishes you with the essence of knowledge, molds your brain to a technical background, and catechizes you about the world of INDHAN (Petroleum and Chemical Engineering). Come join us through our memorandum, qualify through different rounds, and be a winner.

Event Format

There are four rounds in this event

Round 1 (Paper based test)

  • Consists of 20 bits(contains questions on reasoning and basics of core subjects)
  • Top 9 Teams having the highest score will be eligible for the next round

Round 2

  • 2 questions based on the core subjects and one true or false question will be given(no transfer of question to other team)
  • 2 Teams will be eliminated in this round

Round 3 (Picture round)

  • 3 Pictures will be given for each team
  • 2 Teams will be eliminated

    • Round 4 (Rapid fire round)

    • Team members should answer the questions within the time they have been allotted

    Scoring Pattern

    • +3 for correct answer
    • -1 for incorrect answer

    Time Limit

    • 30 seconds for your question
    • No time will be given for transferred question
    • 1 minute for rapid fire round for each team

    Rules:

    • Usage of mobile phones is highly prohibited during this event
    • Candidates who doesn't follow the rules will be disqualified from the event

    Registration guidelines:

    1.Registration of this event Should be made besides basic registration.
    2. Registration fee to participate in the contest is Rs.200/-(2 Participants).

    The dead line for registration is: 23-03-2023
