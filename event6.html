EXPERIRI

About:

Welcome to the world of model representation! In this brochure, we will introduce you to model presentation event , EXPERIRI. Whether you are a student just beginning to learn about chemistry or a chemical engineering student, chemical models are an essential part of your toolkit. So why not dive in and start exploring the fascinating world of model representation today?

Hurry up and don't miss out on the opportunity to register for our upcoming event! Registrations opening soon. This is your chance to be a part of a dynamic and engaging event that will provide you with valuable knowledge and skills.

So what are you waiting for? Register now and be a part of this amazing event! We look forward to seeing you there.

Rules:

  • The models can be made depicting latest topics , like solar energy, fuels for the future, industrial safety etc or any other topic related to chemical engineering and petroleum engineering.
  • The students are required to bring their representative models along with them.
  • The students are required to submit their model abstract prior to the event.

Registration guidelines:

  • Registration of this event Should be made besides basic registration.
  • Registration fee to participate in the contest is Rs.300/- (1-4 Participants).

    • The dead line for registration is: 23-03-2023
