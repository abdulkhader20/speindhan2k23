POSTERA (Poster Making Contest)

About:

As a part of SPE INDHAN 2023-We invite you to participate in technical poster making contest. The objective behind poster making competition is to bring out the creative expression of students & increases the observation skills, thoughts, awareness and concern of the students. Hence, we are giving a challenge to represent your ideas, thoughts, knowledge through your posters.

GuideLines:

Participants are invited to present a technical poster on topics related to engineering streams

Petroleum Engineering:

  • Advances in Drilling and Completions
  • Advances in Production and Reservoir Engineering
  • Tight Gas Reservoirs
  • Subsea Engineering and Technology
  • HSE in Oil and Gas Industry
  • Flow Assurance
  • Digitiation & Automation in Oil & Gas Industry
  • Climatic change

Chemical Engineering:

  • Process Integration
  • Carbon Sequestration
  • Fuels of the Future
  • Production of Green Chemicals
  • Utilization of plastic and polymetrics.
  • Solar Energy & and Its Application in Chemical Process Industry
  • Industrial Safety and Hazard Management
  • Digitization and Automation in Chemical Process Industry

Rules:

  • Each Participant can post only one Poster.
  • The poster must be made on the size (6x4 feet) flex sheet with margins (0.5 inches) drawn with black pen/marker.
  • Poster should not contain any personal details like Name, Class, College etc. of the participants.
  • These details should be mentioned in registration form only.
  • The Topic of poster and theme should be clearly mentioned at the Top Margin of the Poster.
  • Abstract should not be more than 1000 words, formatted in Microsoft Word and single-spaced, using size 12 with Times New Roman Font to explain the topic and content of the poster to be sent our mail id speindhan2k23@gmail.com Irrelevant or non technical posters will leads to disqualification.

Registration guidelines:

Registration of this event Should be made besides basic registration.
Registration fee to participate in the contest is Free-(2 Participants).


The dead line for registration is: 23-03-2023

Rewards:

The Winner of the Contest will get an exciting reward along with a JNTUK certificate.
