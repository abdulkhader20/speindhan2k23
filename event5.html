CHEM-E-CROSS

About:

Chemical engineering that deals with developing and designing chemical manufacturing processes. And the person responsible for all these operations is called a chemical engineer. There are many technical terms in this field about which students should be aware. For the same purpose, we have created this crossword puzzle that will help you to expand your knowledge about chemical engineering. Step forward and earn your place in CHEM-E-CROSS event.

Rules:

  • Phones or electrical gadgets should not be allowed
  • There will be 2 rounds in which participants who got their round 1 crossword in less time with correct answers will be selected to next round
  • No need to carry any stationary. Required stationary will be provided by the coordinators All answers that are present in the crossword should be single word
  • Hints will be provided for the given crossword

Registration guidelines:

  • Registration of this event should be made besides basic registration.
  • Registration fee to participate in the contest is Rs.100/- (1 Participants).

    • The dead line for registration is: 23-03-2023
