🧩 Enigma

Engineering Problem Solving Challenge

About

Enigma is a challenging problem-solving event that tests your engineering knowledge, lateral thinking, and ability to crack complex technical puzzles. Think you can solve the unsolvable? Come prove it.

Format

  • Multiple rounds of increasing difficulty
  • Technical puzzles from petroleum and chemical engineering
  • Logical reasoning and aptitude challenges
  • Team-based problem solving

Registration

Registration must be made in addition to basic registration.

⏰ Registration Deadline: 23 March 2023

Rewards

Winners will receive exciting rewards along with JNTUK certificates.
