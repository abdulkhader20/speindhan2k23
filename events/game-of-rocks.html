🪨 Game of Rocks

Rock Identification & Petrophysics Competition

About

Game of Rocks is a rock identification and petrophysics-based competition that tests your geology and reservoir engineering knowledge. Can you identify rock types, interpret core samples, and solve petrophysics problems?

What to Expect

  • Rock and mineral identification challenges
  • Core sample analysis
  • Petrophysics calculations and interpretation
  • Reservoir characterization problems

Registration

Registration must be made in addition to basic registration.

⏰ Registration Deadline: 23 March 2023

Rewards

Winners will receive exciting rewards along with JNTUK certificates.
