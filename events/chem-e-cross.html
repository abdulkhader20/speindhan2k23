🔀 Chem-E-Cross

Engineering Crossword Puzzle Competition

About

Chem-E-Cross is a crossword puzzle competition based on chemical and petroleum engineering concepts. Test your vocabulary and technical knowledge in this fun yet challenging event.

Format

  • Crossword puzzles with engineering terminology
  • Covers both chemical and petroleum engineering topics
  • Timed rounds
  • Individual or team participation

Registration

Registration must be made in addition to basic registration.

⏰ Registration Deadline: 23 March 2023

Rewards

Winners will receive exciting rewards along with JNTUK certificates.
