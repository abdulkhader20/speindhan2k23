🛢️ Mud-Mash

Drilling Fluid Preparation & Testing Competition

About

Mud-Mash is a drilling fluid (mud) preparation and testing competition exclusively for petroleum engineering students. Participants will prepare drilling mud formulations and test their properties — a true test of your petroleum engineering fundamentals.

What You'll Do

  • Prepare drilling fluid formulations
  • Test mud properties: density, viscosity, filtration
  • Optimize formulations for given well conditions
  • Present and defend your formulation choices

Registration

Registration must be made in addition to basic registration.

⏰ Registration Deadline: 23 March 2023

Rewards

Winners will receive exciting rewards along with JNTUK certificates.
