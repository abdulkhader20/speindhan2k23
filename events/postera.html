🖼️ Postera

Technical Poster Making Contest

About

As a part of SPE INDHAN 2023, we invite you to participate in a technical poster making contest. The objective is to bring out the creative expression of students and increase observation skills, thoughts, awareness and concern. We challenge you to represent your ideas, thoughts, and knowledge through your posters.

Guidelines — Petroleum Engineering Topics

  • Advances in Drilling and Completions
  • Advances in Production and Reservoir Engineering
  • Tight Gas Reservoirs
  • Subsea Engineering and Technology
  • HSE in Oil and Gas Industry
  • Flow Assurance
  • Digitization & Automation in Oil & Gas Industry
  • Climatic Change

Guidelines — Chemical Engineering Topics

  • Process Integration
  • Carbon Sequestration
  • Fuels of the Future
  • Production of Green Chemicals
  • Utilization of Plastics and Polymers
  • Solar Energy & Its Application in Chemical Process Industry
  • Industrial Safety and Hazard Management
  • Digitization and Automation in Chemical Process Industry

Rules

  • Each participant can submit only one poster.
  • Poster must be made on 6×4 feet flex sheet with 0.5 inch margins drawn with black pen/marker.
  • Poster should NOT contain any personal details (name, class, college) — these go in the registration form only.
  • The topic and theme must be clearly mentioned at the top margin.
  • Abstract must not exceed 1000 words, formatted in MS Word, single-spaced, Times New Roman size 12.
  • Send abstract to: speindhan2k23@gmail.com
  • Irrelevant or non-technical posters will lead to disqualification.

Registration

Registration must be made in addition to basic registration.

Registration Fee: Free (2 Participants)

⏰ Registration Deadline: 23 March 2023

Rewards

The winner will receive an exciting reward along with a JNTUK certificate.
