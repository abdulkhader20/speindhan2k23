⚗️ Titro

Titration-Based Chemistry Competition

About

Titro is a hands-on titration-based chemistry competition that tests your laboratory skills and analytical thinking. Participants will perform titration experiments under timed conditions and will be judged on accuracy, technique, and results.

Who Can Participate

Open to students from Chemical Engineering and Petroleum Engineering departments from any college across India.

What to Expect

  • Acid-base titration experiments
  • Redox titration challenges
  • Timed rounds with increasing difficulty
  • Judged on accuracy and lab technique

Registration

Registration must be made in addition to basic registration.

⏰ Registration Deadline: 23 March 2023

Rewards

Winners will receive exciting rewards along with JNTUK certificates.
