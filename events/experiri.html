🧪 Experiri

Practical Engineering Skills Event

About

Experiri (Latin for "to try/experience") is an experimental event where participants demonstrate their practical engineering skills. Get hands-on with real engineering challenges and show what you've learned beyond the classroom.

Format

  • Hands-on practical experiments
  • Real engineering problem scenarios
  • Judged on accuracy, methodology, and presentation
  • Team participation encouraged

Registration

Registration must be made in addition to basic registration.

⏰ Registration Deadline: 23 March 2023

Rewards

Winners will receive exciting rewards along with JNTUK certificates.
