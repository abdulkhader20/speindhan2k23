💡 Technovation

PowerPoint Presentation Contest

About

"KNOWLEDGE is knowing what to say, WISDOM is knowing when to say it"


TECHNOVATION is a platform to present your knowledge on various chemical and petroleum concepts and share innovative ideas. SPE INDHAN 2023 brings you this PowerPoint presentation contest that provides a platform to exhibit and share your technical and interpersonal skills.

Topics — Petroleum Engineering

  • Advances in Drilling and Completions
  • Advances in Production and Reservoir Engineering
  • Tight Gas Reservoirs
  • Subsea Engineering and Technology
  • HSE in Oil and Gas Industry
  • Flow Assurance
  • Digitization & Automation in Oil & Gas Industry
  • Climatic Change

Topics — Chemical Engineering

  • Process Integration
  • Carbon Sequestration
  • Fuels of the Future
  • Production of Green Chemicals
  • Utilization of Plastics and Polymers
  • Solar Energy & Its Application in Chemical Process Industry
  • Industrial Safety and Hazard Management
  • Digitization and Automation in Chemical Process Industry

Rules

  • Abstract must contain participant name, branch, college, email, and contact number.
  • Abstract should be informative and below 1000 words.
  • Send abstract to: speindhan2k23@gmail.com
  • Acceptance of abstract will be intimated.
