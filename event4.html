ENIGMA (Puzzle)

About:

This event highlights the design of instrumentations. The design of the game is meant to teach basic concepts of chemical related equipment by rearranging the puzzle. The game tries to interact fun with education .
Step forward and earn your place in enigma event


Rules:

  • Jigsaw puzzles are to be arranged by the pictures shown by the organizers.
  • The Pictures provided are only the equipment's of the chemical and petroleum engineering laboratories.
  • One who correctly arranges the jigsaw puzzle (of equipment's) will be qualified to next level of the event
  • If any two students are at the same level of the winning opportunity, then time factor is considered.
  • Mobile phones are strictly not allowed at this event.
  • Hints are not entertained, only pictures are shown.

Registration guidelines:

Registration of this event Should be made besides basic registration.
Registration fee to participate in the contest is Rs.200/-(2 Participants).


The dead line for registration is: 23-03-2023

Rewards:

The Winner of the Contest will get an exciting reward along with a JNTUK certificate.
