🎓 Campus Ambassador

Be the face of SPE INDHAN 2K23 at your college

What is a Campus Ambassador?

A Campus Ambassador is a student representative who promotes SPE INDHAN 2K23 at their college, encourages participation, and acts as a point of contact between their college and the organizing team.

✅ Responsibilities

  • Promote SPE INDHAN 2K23 at your college
  • Encourage students to register and participate
  • Share event updates and information
  • Coordinate with the organizing team
  • Help outstation participants with logistics

🎁 Benefits

  • Official Campus Ambassador certificate from JNTUK
  • Free registration for SPE INDHAN 2K23
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Priority access to workshops and events
  • Recognition on the official website and social media
