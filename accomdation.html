Accomodation

We, at SPE INDHAN 2023, constantly strive towards the satisfaction of everyone. We are fulfilling the needs of a safe & secure accommodation away from home, within low cost. Along with accommodation facilities for our participants, we also set up a cafeteria serving a variety of cuisines satisfying the needs of every palate. We strive to make your stay comfortable and your experience, a memorable one. Hospitality management would be one of the prime focuses of Team SPE INDHAN 2023 .

INSTRUCTIONS

1. All guests will be provided with mattress. SPE INDHAN 2023 will not provide mattress cover, blankets or pillows. The guests are encouraged to arrange them on their own (if required), since the weather might get cold during the night.
2. Random checks would be made to avoid any illegal stay at the campus. Any team failing to produce their electronic/physical receipts of accommodation would be heavily fined and disqualified.
3. The student participants are required to carry their valid College photo id card.
Any guest failing to produce their id card will not be permitted inside the campus during SPE INDHAN 2023 .
4. Alcohol, drugs, sharp objects and explosives of any kind are strictly prohibited inside the campus. Any other item if deemed unsafe will be prohibited. The decision of Security and SPE INDHAN 2023 team will be final in case of any disputes.
5. All guests are required to maintain the decorum and cleanliness of the campus, and follow the rules of the campus at all times.
6. SPE INDHAN 2023 will not be responsible for any mishaps that occur through the duration of stay.
7. All those who does not have accommodation inside the campus, will not be allowed to stay in the campus after 10 P.M.
8. Only the guests having their accommodation in the campus will be allowed in SPE INDHAN 2023 is awaiting your presence.

Registration Fee Details

Accommodation charges are Rs 100 (inclusive of all taxes) per candidate per day.
Maximum of 2 nights stay allowed (24th March - 26th March).
