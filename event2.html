TECHNOVATION

About:

"KNOWLEDGE is knowing what to say, WISDOM is knowing when to say it"

TECHNOVATION (Power Point Presentation) is a platform to present your knowledge on various chemical and petroleum concepts and share innovative ideas with us. This proposes the curiosity of new minds to explore by conveying their innovativeness. SPE INDHAN 2023 brings you TECHNOVATION (a PowerPoint presentation) that provides you a platform to exhibit and share your technical and interpersonal skills.

GuideLines:

Participants are invited to present a technical poster on topics related to engineering streams

Petroleum Engineering:

1. Advances in Drilling and Completions
2. Advances in Production and Reservoir Engineering
3. Tight Gas Reservoirs
4. Subsea Engineering and Technology
5. HSE in Oil and Gas Industry
6. Flow Assurance
7. Digitiation & Automation in Oil & Gas Industry
8. Climatic change

Chemical Engineering:

1. Process Integration
2. Carbon Sequestration
3. Fuels of the Future
4. Production of Green Chemicals
5. Utilization of plastic and polymetrics.
6. Solar Energy & and Its Application in Chemical Process Industry
7. Industrial Safety and Hazard Management
8. Digitization and Automation in Chemical Process Industry

Rules:

  • Abstract must contain name of the participant, branch name, college name, email address and contact number.
  • Abstract should be informative and contain below 1000 words.
  • Candidates are requested to send the abstract of your presentation to the Email Id Speindhan2k23@gmail.com
  • Acceptance of abstract will be intimated.
